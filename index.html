Who's Sam, our chef?
After an apprenticeship in his native region of Aveyron in South Western France, Sam Legratt made his way to Paris in 2000 to work in the kitchen of L'Arpège, the restaurant of Chef Alain Passard. He honed his skills alongside the Jacques and Laurent Pourcel brothers at La Maison Blancheand Le Jardin des Sens.
